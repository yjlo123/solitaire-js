Cards can be stacked and moved only if they are of alternating colors and in decreasing order (K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6).

The four free cells can store one card or a completed stack.

Dragon cards can be stacked and moved to the dragon cell.

To win, sort the cards into four completed stacks and move them to the free cells.
